Why Choose Us

Jenney’s College of Education has a longstanding legacy of providing quality Education & Training in the field of Education. With a rich history & a reputation for excellence, the college has been producing highly skilled educators for many years.

The college boasts a team of experienced & dedicated faculty who are experts in their respective fields.

Jenney’s College of Education is committed to the ongoing professional Development of its students. The college provides various workshops, seminars & training programmes to enhance teaching skills & pedagogy & subject specific expertise, keeping students up-to-date with the latest trends & best practices in education.

College maintains a strong network of Alumini who have gone on to achieve success on various educational settings. This network serves as a valuable resource for current students offering mentorship, networking opportunities & career guidance.

About Us

Why Choose Us

Srimathi Rani Adaikalaraj Educational & charitable Trust was created by our honorable founder Shri L.Adaikalaraj (Late), the former M.P. from Tiruchirappalli constituency. The present Managing Trustee & Correspondent of Jenneys Group of Institutions and JENNEY’S COLLEGE OF EDUCATION, Mr. A. Joseph Francis, a man of simplicity but a dynamic personality with the twin views of Education for the Masses and serving the society through education. Our young Secretary Mr. J.F.Antony Siddhant, a young enterprising and energetic personality is ably managing and successfully promoting Jenney’s Group of Institutions.

To champion the cause of justice & human dignity and to overcome emerging challenges of society, our Trust started JENNEY’S COLLEGE OF EDUCATION in the year 2007 at Tiruchirappalli for young student Teachers. Jenney’s college of Education is approved by the National council for Teachers Education and affiliated to Tamil Nadu Teachers Education University, Chennai.
