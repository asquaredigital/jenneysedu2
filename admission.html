Admission Procedure

The completed application form for admission to B.Ed. & M.Ed. courses shall be sent to the principal. After proper screening and evaluation of marks, the candidates are provisionally selected for admission. The following original certificates with 3 sets of xerox copies should be produced by the candidates at the time of admission. Incomplete applications will summarily be rejected.

List of Certificates

  • Transfer certificate.

  • Conduct certificate.

  • SSLC Mark sheet.

  • HSC Mark sheet.

  • UG Degree Mark sheet.

  • UG Degree/Provisional sheet.

  • PG Degree Mark sheet.

  • PG Degree/Provisional certificate.

  • B.Ed. Degree Mark sheet (For M.Ed.).

  • B.Ed. Degree/Provisional certificate (For M.Ed.).

  • Community certificate.

  • Passport size photos 10 in Nos.

Student Services

For the effective nurturing, growth and development of the student trainees our college renders student services. This is of prime importance as it helps students to develop their personality, meet the needs, and excel in the dynamic global environment.

Career Counselling

Students are given consistent assistance and guidance to decide their career path. They need directives as regard to choose the schools during the campus interview to streamline and channelize their efforts towards securing a bright future.

Remedial Coaching

It helps students to learn good learning habits and attitudes. Course teachers arrange remedial coaching for slow learners in all the programmes. This extra coaching helps them to come out of the difficulties in learning and to fill the cognitive gaps and to cope with the challenges of higher education.

Coaching for TET/NET

The college arranges coaching for student teachers to crack the Teacher Eligibility Test and also offers coaching for M.Ed. students to break through National Eligibility Test.

Health care service

The college provides health care services to all students at any time during college hours. The sick room is equipped with a bed, first aid kit and medicines for primary treatment. Students who require immediate medical help are taken to the nearby hospital, which is in the next door.
